ABOUT US

"You can't buy happiness, but you can adopt it".

Welcome to PET~SET HOME, where every pet has a story and every story deserves a happy ending.

At PET~SET HOME, we are passionate for the well-being of animals. Founded with a deep love for animals and a desire to make a meaningful difference in their lives, we are dedicated to rescuing, nurturing, and ultimately finding loving families for pets in need.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that every pet deserves a chance at happiness and security. Guided by this principle, we work tirelessly to provide refuge, medical care, and socialization to countless animals, regardless of breed, age, or background.

At PET~SET HOME, we take pride in our commitment to transparency, integrity, and ethical practices. From our thorough adoption process to our comprehensive post-adoption support, we prioritize the well-being of both our animals and their future families.

But our impact extends beyond the walls of our shelter. Through community partnerships, educational programs, and advocacy efforts.
